welcome to Honey Treat Academy

Honeytreat Trade Academy is a private vocational training institute in construction trades, aimed at helping the youth, existing construction trade workers and artisans acquire perfect skills, compe tence, and certifications in their various trades for better economic benefits.

the leading global marketplace for learning and instruction

We have set out to provide the needed internationally ac ceptable training in the vocational sector in Nigeria. We have been achieving this using a blended learning program which is a combination of classroom-styled teaching, hands-on practical sessions and extensive exposure to rele vant technology. Coming from the experience of Honeytr eat Limited, the mother company to HTA, we have been in the built environment for over a decade. We are passionate to see quality service per ex cellence delivered to individuals and companies in the countr y.

We understand the need for prompt access to qualified, mannered and competent artisans. Also, as a body that has turned out hundreds of trained artisans in construction trades, we understand what qualifies an artisan for a job. Therefore, we took up this task to ensure that the right person is on the job and that every individual and company has their need met by competent artisans.

To build good professional competence, attitude and integrity in construction trade workers.

To have a society where construction workers in Nigeria can compete favorably and achieve better productivity than those in developed countries.

To build an establishment that will provide competent construction workforce for Africa first and then the World.
